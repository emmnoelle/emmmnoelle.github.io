EMMA NOELLE // PAGE ABOUT MYSELF

I am a first year student studying at the Cleveland Institute Art enrolled in the Graphic Design major. I have always been drawn to the concept that art is just a form of communication. It was this concept that sparked my interest in graphic design.

I enjoy working with expressive typography, coding, and digital art as well as traditional mediums such as painting or pen and ink. I'm currently exploring design as it pertains to marketing, branding, and interior architecture.
